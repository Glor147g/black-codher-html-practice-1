I found my thrill on Blueberry Hill. Fats Domino

This cake is simply the most delicious fruit infused sponge you'll ever need. Blueberry has that jamlike quality but holds its shape while leaving a lovely colour. Plus it a cinch to decorate with a tasty buttercream. Try a simple one-to-one ratio and you'll have time for a laid back treat. Key Ingredients Include:

  • Blueberries(seasonal if possible)
  • Buttermilk (but yogurt and lemon will do)
  • Plain flour (but Self-Raising works too)
